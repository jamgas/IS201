Great Reasons to get a PhD


  • The best reason to get a PhD is because you are intellectually curious about EVERYTHING, or about something in particular. As a scientist (another name for a research professor), you will be constantly coming up with and pursuing your own ideas and the ideas of others. Being intellectually curious will be something that helps drive your desire to continue to do research, and will help you find areas of study.
  • You will thrive best if you are deeply passionate about ideas and knowledge. This does not mean you have to thoroughly enjoy reading academic articles... but it does mean that studying and working to discover more about different ideas and areas of knowledge needs to be something that you love.
  • Another good peripheral reason to get a PhD is because you want to have the autonomy to dictate your own career and trajectory. Getting a PhD is a great way to keep a wide-open venue of career options, and prevents you from backing yourself into a corner. While research should still be something you enjoy doing, this is a great way to keep doors open for many career options.
  • Wanting to have a job that enables you to be an available spouse and parent (if this is in your future), should not be your only reason but can be a good reason -and great motivation- to pursue the further education. The work-life balance of a professor and of other opportunities provided by receiving a PhD makes it possible to have a wonderful family life.

Bad Reasons


  • Not being married yet and wanting to stick around school to find a spouse. Marriage is a great goal, but successful PhD's are more likely to be a result of the reasons above.
  • Being afraid to leave school or hesitant to find a full-time "real" job are not good reasons to start on a PhD.
  • Being scared of the real world is not a good reason. Deciding to get a PhD needs to be what you want to do, not a result of avoiding anything else.
  • Wanting more time to play video games...
  • Wanting more free time or more money. There are many easier ways to make a good living and to have a good schedule.
